Business: SkillBridge Academy

Business Description:
SkillBridge Academy is an online learning academy for students, fresh graduates, and early-career professionals who want practical job-ready technology skills.

Supported Courses:
1. Python Programming
2. Data Analytics
3. Generative AI Basics
4. Full-Stack Web Development
5. Interview Preparation Bootcamp

Course Details:
Python Programming:
- Fee: ₹4,999
- Duration: 6 weeks
- Level: Beginner-friendly
- Includes: Python basics, functions, OOP, file handling, APIs, mini projects

Data Analytics:
- Fee: ₹6,999
- Duration: 8 weeks
- Level: Beginner to intermediate
- Includes: Excel, SQL basics, Python for analysis, Pandas, visualization, dashboard basics

Generative AI Basics:
- Fee: ₹7,999
- Duration: 4 weeks
- Level: Beginner-friendly for learners with basic Python knowledge
- Includes: LLM basics, prompting, RAG introduction, LangChain basics, simple AI app project

Full-Stack Web Development:
- Fee: ₹8,999
- Duration: 10 weeks
- Level: Beginner to intermediate
- Includes: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, FastAPI basics, database integration, deployment basics

Interview Preparation Bootcamp:
- Fee: ₹2,999
- Duration: 2 weeks
- Level: Suitable for freshers and early-career candidates
- Includes: resume review, mock interview, project explanation practice, HR questions, technical Q&A practice

Batch Timings:
- Weekday evening batch: Monday to Friday, 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM
- Weekend morning batch: Saturday and Sunday, 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM
- New batches usually start every Monday for short courses and every first Saturday for weekend batches

Mode of Learning:
- All courses are online and instructor-led
- Live sessions are conducted through video meeting links
- Session recordings are available for enrolled learners for 30 days

Certificate:
- Certificate of completion is provided after attending at least 70% of sessions and submitting required assignments

Enrollment:
- Students can enroll through the SkillBridge website or WhatsApp admissions team
- Payment must be completed before batch confirmation
- The admissions team usually confirms enrollment within 24 working hours after payment

Placement and Career Support:
- SkillBridge provides resume review, mock interviews, project explanation guidance, and job application guidance
- SkillBridge does not guarantee jobs, interviews, salary packages, or placement outcomes

Refund and Cancellation:
- Refund requests must be reviewed by the admissions team
- The AI assistant must not approve, reject, or promise refunds
- Batch change requests are handled by the admissions team based on seat availability

Payment:
- Accepted payment methods: UPI, debit card, credit card, and net banking
- EMI options are not mentioned in the SOP
- Scholarships or discounts are not mentioned in the SOP

Escalation Rules:
Escalate to a human admissions counselor if:
- The learner asks for a refund, cancellation, scholarship, discount, EMI, or payment exception
- The learner reports payment failure or duplicate payment
- The learner complains about trainer quality, certificate delay, access issues, or missed recordings
- The learner is angry, frustrated, or dissatisfied
- The learner asks for guaranteed placement, guaranteed interview calls, or salary promises
- The learner asks for a custom corporate training plan
- The learner asks something not covered in this SOP
- The AI cannot answer more than two questions from the SOP
- The learner explicitly asks to speak to a counselor, human, manager, or trainer

Lead Qualification Questions:
To qualify a course enquiry, collect:
1. Interested course
2. Learning goal
3. Current skill level: beginner, intermediate, or advanced
4. Learner type: student, fresher, working professional, career switcher, or business owner
5. Preferred batch: weekday evening or weekend morning
6. Timeline to start: immediately, this month, or later
7. Phone or email for admissions follow-up, if the learner voluntarily provides it

Recommended Next Actions:
- If the learner is interested in a listed course and asks normal course questions, answer from the SOP and continue qualification
- If the learner is unsure which course to choose, ask about their learning goal and current skill level
- If the learner wants enrollment, collect qualification details and recommend contacting the WhatsApp admissions team or website enrollment
- If the learner asks for refund, discount, EMI, job guarantee, complaint handling, or unsupported details, escalate to a human admissions counselor
